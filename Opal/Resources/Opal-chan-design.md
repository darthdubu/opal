# Opal-chan - Anime Mascot Design

## Character Concept
**Name:** Opal-chan (オパルちゃん)
**Theme:** Cute terminal hacker girl inspired by actual opal gemstones - the precious stone with "play-of-color"

## Opal Gemstone Inspiration
Real opals feature:
- **Base:** Milky white, translucent body
- **Play-of-Color:** Rainbow flashes that shift with movement (red, orange, yellow, green, blue, violet)
- **Patterns:** Harlequin, pinfire, or flash patterns
- **Glow:** Internal iridescence that seems to come from within

## Appearance
- **Hair:** Long flowing white/platinum hair with **play-of-color streaks** - strands that shift through rainbow hues (red→orange→yellow→green→blue→violet) as she moves, mimicking opal's color flash
- **Eyes:** Large luminous eyes with **milky white base and internal rainbow iridescence**, like looking into an opal gemstone. The colors shift and shimmer.
- **Skin:** Slight pearlescent glow with subtle opalescence
- **Outfit:** 
  - **Hoodie:** White/cream base made to look like **milky opal texture** with translucent quality
  - Rainbow circuit lines that pulse with opal's play-of-color
  - Hood shaped like an **opal cabochon** (rounded dome shape)
  - LED patterns mimic **opal flash patterns** (harlequin/pinfire)
  - **Skirt:** Gradient layers that look like opal's color bands
- **Pose:** Standing gracefully, holding out her hand palm-up presenting a **large, glowing opal gemstone** that floats slightly above her palm
  - The opal gemstone is the **actual app icon shape** - a rounded square with play-of-color
  - Light from the gemstone illuminates her face with rainbow hues
- **Terminal Integration in Clothing:**
  - **Hoodie front:** The entire front panel is a **holographic terminal window display** showing actual code/terminal output that glows
  - **Sleeves:** Cuff areas have **LED command prompts** that pulse (showing "~$" or "opal>")
  - **Hood lining:** Inside of hood shows **scrolling terminal text** when viewed from certain angles
  - **Skirt patterns:** Pleats form the shape of **terminal window frames** when viewed from the front
  - **Belt/ribbon:** A sash across her waist made of **glowing command line interface** with blinking cursor
- **Accessories:**
  - **Floating holographic terminal windows** orbit around her like magical familiar screens
  - **Keyboard keys made of tiny opal chips** float around her fingertips
  - When she types, **rainbow opal sparkles** emanate from her fingers
  - **Headphones:** Resemble **old CRT monitor casings** with glowing screens on the ear cups

## Personality
- Energetic and friendly terminal companion
- Speaks in terminal commands ("cd ~/happiness" "sudo make-me-smile")
- Loves coding and helping users
- Gets excited when terminal processes complete successfully

## Icon Versions

### App Icon (Dock - 16x16 to 512x512px)
**Simplified portrait design for small sizes:**
- Close-up portrait showing face and upper torso only
- **Focus:** Her opal eyes (most recognizable feature at small scale)
- **Hood:** Simplified shape with subtle terminal window pattern (reads as texture at small sizes)
- **Gemstone:** Large opal floating near her face/shoulder, NOT held in hand (hands are too small for icons)
- **Colors:** Limited palette - white/cream base with rainbow opal accents
- **Background:** Simple gradient or solid color to make silhouette readable at 16px
- **Style:** Clean lines, readable silhouette, maximum 3-4 colors in small sizes

### Full Body Illustration (Marketing/Splash)
**Detailed artwork with all elements:**
- Complete pose holding the opal gemstone in palm
- All terminal clothing details visible
- Holographic windows and floating elements
- Complex opal textures and lighting effects
